About ABPA

Serving the pensioners of Antigua & Barbuda since our founding

Our Mission

The Antigua and Barbuda Pensioners Association (ABPA) is dedicated to protecting the rights, interests, and well-being of all retired workers across our beautiful twin-island nation.

Founded with the vision of creating a strong, united voice for pensioners, we work tirelessly to ensure that the contributions of our senior citizens are recognized, respected, and properly rewarded. Our association serves as a bridge between retired workers and the institutions that affect their lives.

Our Objectives

  • Advocacy: Representing pensioners' interests at local and national levels
  • Support: Providing guidance and assistance with pension-related matters
  • Community: Building a strong network of retired professionals
  • Education: Keeping members informed about their rights and benefits
  • Wellness: Promoting health and well-being among our members

What We Do

Advocacy

We work with government agencies, employers, and other stakeholders to ensure fair treatment of pensioners.

Support Services

Our team provides guidance on pension claims, benefits, and navigating bureaucratic processes.

Social Events

We organize regular meetings, outings, and social gatherings to foster community bonds.

Health Programs

We promote wellness through health fairs, fitness programs, and medical awareness campaigns.

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1995
  • Members: 500+
  • Location: St. John's, Antigua
  • Coverage: Antigua & Barbuda
