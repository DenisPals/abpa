Comprehensive Benefits

Advocacy & Support

We work tirelessly to protect and enhance your pension rights through various channels:

  • Government Representation: Regular meetings with government officials to discuss pension policies
  • Legal Assistance: Access to legal advice and support for pension-related issues
  • Policy Monitoring: Tracking legislative changes that affect pensioners
  • Dispute Resolution: Help resolving pension-related disputes with employers
  • Information Sharing: Regular updates on pension law changes and their impact
Recent Achievements
  • Successfully lobbied for pension indexing
  • Improved healthcare benefits for pensioners
  • Reduced processing times for pension claims
  • Enhanced survivor benefits
